My travels throughout New Zealand

How we did New Zealand


South Island: Christchurch to Queenstown

When booking our trip to New Zealand we explored many options on flights and drives. We landed on flying into Christchurch and driving to Queenstown on the South Island. After flying to Christchurch, we rented a car and drove to Lake Tekapo where we camped overnight lake side. We stayed in a "glamping" style hostel and absolutely loved it. Sunrise the next day we drove the rest of the distance to Queenstown where we stayed at the Jucy Snooze. In New Zealand we did not waste time and sought out any adventure acitivites. We went skydiving, jet boating, ludging, hiking, white water rafting and more. We ended our nights bar hopping throughout the city! It was a must see in my abroad experience.

Activities


Jet Boating

An Hour experience. It was a lot of fun, not too expensive and more thrilling than we expected. 3/5 thrill level


White Water Rafting

This was a four hour experience. They provided all of the necessary equipment and transportation to the destination. We rode on the most dangerous road in New Zealand, Skipper's Canyon and got to drive into the mountains. We rafted down the Shotover River and saw many goats and other New Zealand animals. We choose the multiple rapid option due to the ability of our group. Thrill level 5/5.


Gondola

We rode the gondola up to the activites at the top of the mountain. Although the gondola was not very long or much of a thrill, I would suggest taking a ride to see the view of Queenstown from the top.


Ludging

This activity was fun a couple times down however, 15 minutes at this was plenty. Thrill level 2/5.
